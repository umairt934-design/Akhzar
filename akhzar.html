How Akhzar
Works

Three steps  ·  Thirty days
01
🌿

Day 1 onwards

Collect Daily

Add your vegetable peels, fruit scraps, tea leaves, and leftover food into the Akhzar kitchen bin. Sprinkle the smell control sachet on top. Takes 30 seconds. That is your entire daily routine.

02
🔄

Every 3 days

Layer and Mix

Transfer waste to the compost container. Add dry leaves or a handful of soil. Give it a mix. No expertise needed. The microbes in the soil do the rest while you go about your day.

Your waste is now rich, dark, earthy compost. Mix it into your plant pots or garden beds. Watch the difference in your plants. No fertilizer purchase needed ever again.
